About FoodPrint

FoodPrint helps people understand and reduce the environmental impact of their meals. Our goal is to make sustainable food choices simple, rewarding, and easy to track.

Our Mission

Food choices have a real impact on the planet. FoodPrint gives users a simple way to log meals, view their estimated carbon footprint, and discover opportunities to make lower-impact choices over time. It is our belief that applications such as ours are necessary to help transition into a low-carbon society in the UK and beyond.

What FoodPrint Does

  • Track meals and estimate food-related emissions.
  • Allow users to compete with each other on our sustainability leaderboard.
  • Encourage lower-impact food choices with tips and tricks specific to you

Why It Matters

Small changes can add up. By helping users understand the impact of everyday meals, FoodPrint supports more informed decisions and encourages long-term sustainable habits.
